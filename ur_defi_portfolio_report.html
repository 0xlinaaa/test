Private Wallet Stablecoin Overview

Key Finding

Of 1,822 UR user private wallets, 124 (6.8%) hold stablecoins totaling $303.1M. These are strictly private wallet balances, excluding any funds deposited into UR EVM accounts. USDC dominates at 71.5% of holdings, followed by USDT at 20.9%. Ethereum holds the majority share at 59.1%, followed by Base (22.1%) and Arbitrum (17.8%).

Section 02
Asset Deployment Breakdown

Deployment Analysis

The overwhelming majority of UR users' private-wallet stablecoins (~98.3%) remain idle — sitting as raw ERC-20 tokens in their wallets. Only approximately ~1.7% ($5.22M) has been actively deployed into lending protocols such as Aave V3, Curve, and Lido over the past 6 months. Meanwhile, DEX trading activity is extremely high ($2.18B volume), indicating users actively swap stablecoins but largely keep base assets idle rather than in yield-generating positions. This represents a significant untapped yield opportunity for the UR ecosystem.
